WHO ARE WE?

The basis of all sorts of art is imagination. The level of expression of imagination defines the beauty of Art. Gestures could not be defined if it had never been noticed. The work of Photographer is to define the iconic gestures. Its like a whole new category in Modern Art. STROKES- the club of Art and Photography presnts before you the game of Rangbaazs. Stay colourful.

OUR FACULTY ADVISOR

Dr. Suvamoy Changder, professor of the Computer Science Engineering Department NITD, famous wildlife photographer as well two times President's award winner is the one who guides us through creating new things through the touch of a brush or the click of a camera.
